Still unsure about a career path that suits you? Here's some information on some
of the most popular and well-paying jobs that you can get

October 25, 2016 | Eliza Lake, Principal

Are you at a point in your life where you're not sure about what you want to do with your life? Whether or not you're entering or leaving high school,
this can be a pretty rough and time-consuming decision. Don't worry. Not everyone knows what they want to be by the time they leave school.
Following your heart is a good thing, but in today's world, an ideal dream job
should be one that you enjoy and can keep you financially stable throughout your life.

You can find more information at http://www.careercast.com/jobs-rated/highest-paying-jobs-2016

Here's a short list of jobs that pay really well in 2016:





1. Surgeon

Annual Median Salary: $357,192

2. Psychiatrist

Annual Median Salary: $207,153

3. Military General

Annual Median Salary: $205,144 (O-10, 25+ years experience)

4. Public Relations Executive (Senior Level)

Annual Median Salary: $201,250

5. Physician (General Practice)

Annual Median Salary: $199,155

6. Corporate Executive (Senior Level)

Annual Median Salary: $173,256

7. Dentist

Annual Median Salary: $155,267

8. Orthodontist

Annual Mean Salary: $131,214

9. Petroleum Engineer

Annual Mean Salary: $129,990

10. Data Scientist

Annual Median Salary: $128,240
