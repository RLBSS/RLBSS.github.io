ABOUT US





THE SCHOOL

A Lively Learning Experience

Here at the Robert L. Bradshaw Secondary School, we offer an exceptional learning environment to our students filled a variety of support and services.
Ever since we got our start in 1980, the Robert L. Bradshaw Secondary School has been instilling knowledge in students in the entire country!
With a staff of over 50 teachers working with over 700 bold, young minds,
we have the ability to give our students the tools needed to grow and succeed.













WHAT WE HAVE TO OFFER

A Variety of Classes for all Careers!

Robert L. Bradshaw Secondary School provides students with a creative and comfortable learning atmosphere with many options for our students.
With many classes available ranging from the hardcore sciences, such as Chemistry and Physics,
to business management in the office, such as Principles of Accounts, Principles of Business and Office Administration,
and even as far as music and art for the creative young minds!

















OUR ACHIEVEMENTS OVER THE YEARS


We respect the individual needs of all our students and believe that with the right guidance and direction, they can all succeed.
Our educational approach empowers our students to hone their skills in both academics and in sports.

Robert L. Bradshaw Secondary School has entered many local competitions with other public schools all throughout the island including
the S.C.A.S.P.A.'s Best In Class High School Quiz Competition and the St. Kitts Nevis Amateur Athletic Association's (S.K.N.A.A.A.)
Inter-school Championships. Competing in the S.C.A.S.P.A.'s Quiz Competition ever since it began in 2013, our students have managed to come in second
place in 2013, third place in 2014 and first place in 2015. Our younger students in primary school have also achieved greatness in the
Our students have truly made great efforts to make their school proud and have won many awards and trophies in both sports and academics!
















OUR MISSION




We desire to create an educational atmosphere that students will love being around. Our programs are intended to empower students to accept challenges and take on experiences that may be new to them.
At the Robert L. Bradshaw Secondary School, we are constantly striving to make learning fun and dynamic so that all of our students can succeed in life
as we have since we were first established.
