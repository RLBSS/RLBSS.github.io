LEARN ABOUT US







The transformative learning experiences at the Robert L. Bradshaw Secondary School are designed to help our students grow both in and out of the classroom. Founded in 1980, the Robert. L Bradshaw Secondary School is located on Williams Street, Basseterre, St. Kitts and Nevis is equipped with modern facilities and reflects the vibrant energy of the federation. With over 700 students and a staff of 50 teachers, we are here to help our students make a positive impact on the world. Are you ready to reach your potential? Join us!
